Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Rubik's Cube app (the "App").

Summary

This App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information of any kind.

Data Collection

The App does not require account creation, login, or registration. The App does not collect, request, or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other data of any kind

The App functions entirely on your device. No data is sent to us or to any third party, because the App does not connect to the internet or any external server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics tools, or other services that collect data.

Children's Privacy

Because the App collects no data whatsoever, it does not knowingly or unknowingly collect any information from children under 13 or any other age.

Data Deletion

Since no data is collected or stored, there is nothing to delete. Uninstalling the App removes it and any local settings from your device entirely.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future (for example, if a future version of the App adds functionality that collects data), this page will be updated accordingly, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
